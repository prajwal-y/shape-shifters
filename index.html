ShapeShifters

Rules

Transform the blue shape to match the red shape by using the transformation buttons. Complete the task in the least number of moves possible.

  • Rotate: Rotates the shape 90 degrees.
  • Scale: Scales the shape by 10%.
  • Reflect: Reflects the shape along the x-axis.
  • Shear: Shears the shape horizontally.
